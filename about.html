THOUGHT PROCESS + CHALLENGES :: DOCUMENTING THE MADNESS WITH RECEIPTS

Thought Process

I am studying away in New York for the first time, and the city hit me hard in the best way. Fast, crowded, a little overwhelming, and genuinely beautiful. This portfolio is my way of holding onto that feeling. Not a tourist postcard, but the energy I actually live in every day. I included all seven sketches because each one marks a step in how I was processing place, identity, and little themes (hearts, light, my initial, nights out).

Visually I leaned into chaotic web energy. Neon contrast, heavy type, ticker text, layered texture, because that matches how loud the city can feel when you are new here. Under that, I kept a simple structure. Shared navigation, cards, and footer so you can always find your way. The gallery is where every piece is named and described in my own words. The playground is where I group sketches on purpose. Love pairs, calm versus spark, and a solo moment for the skyline.
